DECISION & OFFER

  • After your interviews are done, we’ll bring everything together from your application and interviews and review it.
  • We say “we” because we take into account a number of perspectives to come to a decision. If we decide that you’re the best candidate for the role, our team will reach out to you with an offer.
  • Once you’ve accepted the offer, the SGS team will start the onboarding process with you, so you become our new SGS team member.
